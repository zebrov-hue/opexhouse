Data protection

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Opex House Inc. is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit its website and contact us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we use it.

Data Controller

Opex House Inc.
Montréal, Québec, Canada
Email: bonjour@opexhouse.ca

Data We Collect

Through the contact form

When you complete our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your professional email address
  • Your organization name (optional)
  • The content of your message

Through website navigation

Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic technical data (IP address, browser, pages visited) in accordance with its own privacy policy. We do not use tracking cookies or behavioural analytics tools such as Google Analytics.

Purpose of Processing

Data collected through the contact form is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or request for information
  • Schedule a discovery call if requested
  • Send a commercial proposal where relevant

We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

Legal Basis

Processing is based on your explicit consent, given when you submit the contact form, as well as our legitimate interest in responding to inbound inquiries.

Data Retention

Your contact data is retained for as long as necessary to process your request, and for a maximum of 24 months from our last exchange. After this period, it is deleted.

Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • If you become a client of Opex House, certain information may be shared with our operational partners strictly within the scope of the engagement, and under a confidentiality agreement.
  • If required by law or requested by a competent authority.

Your Rights

Under Québec's Act Respecting the Protection of Personal Information in the Private Sector (Law 25) and the federal Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you may request to view the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you may request the correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you may request the deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability — you may request your data in a structured format
  • Right to withdraw consent — you may withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: bonjour@opexhouse.ca

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss or disclosure. Our website uses the HTTPS protocol.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to check this page periodically.
